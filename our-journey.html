Our Journey

Digital Scholar started in Chennai in 2019 as an extension of echoVME (Social Media & Digital Marketing Agency) by Sorav Jain. The creation of Digital Scholar came about with the intention of addressing the graduate-employee skills gap in the industry. We strive to create graduates that seamlessly integrate into the role assigned to them. We focus on building customized teaching patterns that suit the needs of Students, Corporate professionals and Entrepreneurs.

Every batch has not more than 30 students. The curriculum is on par with global standards of digital marketing professionals. We create the perfect blend of classroom learning and on the job training to ensure our students are industry ready from day one after graduation. Students can choose their core interest and opt-in if the offer turns mutually beneficial.

Our Vision

To create the largest conglomeration of digital marketing resources and research opportunities that can benefit the global digital Industry.

Our Mission

  • To imbibe digital culture and create a more talented resources in the domains of Digital Marketing, Digital Analytics, Digital Design and Digital Research.
  • To create Industry Vertical Specific Digital Marketing Programs that can benefit a more niche specialization.
  • To closely work with academicians who have immense interest in Digital Marketing Research to create material that benefits the industry.

Our Approach

Digital Scholar follows a distinct learning approach called the ‘Agency Learning Approach’. Students at Digital Scholar learn as individuals but perform as a group in a completely operational agency environment that we build throughout the journey.

  • Gain practical knowledge and real-time agency expertise
  • Learn to collaborate, brainstorm and bring in more creativity to the table
  • Learn to work within deadlines, handle urgent requirements and inculcate an agency level efficiency into your work
  • Learn to work with coherence as a team with the same synergy
  • Interact with students from different backgrounds, age groups and industries

Founder

In 10+ years of heading my Digital Marketing Agency, I have observed a gap between graduating students’ skill set and the industry requirements. For some reason, our education system could not seamlessly integrate students directly into the Industry or help them take charge of their role right from day one.

I started Digital Scholar with the core idea to bridge this gap. Any graduate from this institute should be empowered to fit into the Industry right from day one. The teaching methods and course curriculum are devised with this idea in mind. Students can work in real-time projects during their course of study empowering them with both experience and confidence when they graduate.

In addition to this, the curriculum is customized for a Solopreneur, Corporate Professional and an Entrepreneur. We group our students to work on their projects based on their future goals.

With more innovation to come I am sure, Digital Scholar will be the go to Institute in India for Digital Marketing.
