Our Digital Marketing Training institute offers 12 modules formulated to impart a holistic understanding of social media and digital marketing. Through these comprehensive modules, the students get to learn as well as apply all aspects of digital marketing on real-time projects.

The modules were curated according to the skill demand in the market and the industry requirements. Each module is handled by a professional with experience and knowledge in the field. Who can Take this Course

Students

Students who are in college or recent graduates who wish to pursue their career in Digital Marketing can apply. This course will lay a solid foundation, provide recognised certifications and help you land a job as a Digital Marketer.

PROFESSIONALS

Professionals working in the Industry who wish to have a profile switch to Digital Marketing or those who seek to add digital expertise to their existing profile can make use of this course and add an array of certifications to your name.

ENTREPRENEURS & FREELANCERS

Freelancers who want to grow their income exponentially and Entrepreneurs who want to understand Digital Marketing to strategize and govern their existing digital marketing activities. Digital Scholar is the right place for you!
